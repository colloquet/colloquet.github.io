About me

Hello, I am Colloque Tsui. I am a Hong Konger who is excited and passionate about web design, my dream is to become a professional web designer. I am currently doing Master in IT at University of Aberdeen, Scotland. Which I also got my Bachelor degree in Accounting and Finance from.

I am proficient in HTML5 and CSS3, as well as some experience with Ruby on Rails and php + MySQL. I am always looking for ways to make things faster, better and stronger.

Skills

Team work and Communication I have developed my team work skill through my university life as well well as summer jobs. Being the treasurer of the Japanese society, I have improved my team work skill. When we organise events, we have to work as a team as it is more effective and efficient. The committee members and I have to meet up and discuss about it. I am responsible for managing the society’s money. If any member of the society wants to use the money on behalf of the society, we will have to discuss about it and see if it is appropriate. In addition to that, we work as a team and we share ideas and suggestions. We then discuss the viability given the resources available to us. Then the president will assign jobs to each of us and we inform each other when we get the job done.
Computing As I am doing MScIT, I am confident to say I possess knowledge about IT that is above average. I am familiar with Microsoft Office as I have used it frequently for my school works. Further more, I have learnt Object Oriented Languages such as PHP and Ruby on Rails. Last but not least, I love web design using HTML, CSS and JS.
Organisation I am a highly organised person, demonstrated through my position in the Japanese Society. As mentioned, I was responsible for the finance of the society. Hence if the account is unbalanced or if I have lost the receipts, I have to fix it using my own money. As a result, I have to be very organised in order to maintain the account. Further more, working in a busy restaurant helped develop these skills further to ensure I worked efficiently and competently under pressure.

Interests

Travel I enjoy travelling as I learn something new every time. I have been to a lot of places and the most remarkable countries for me thus far are Russia and India. I love to explore and experience other cultures. For example, I have been to Delhi for a Punjabi Wedding and it was such a magnificent and amazing experience. I treasure every chance by attentively planning out my trip with my friends.
Sports Football is one of my favourite sports, I often play football with my friends and my position is striker. As a striker, I often have to collaborate with midfielders. This developed the teamwork and communication skills among our teammates.
