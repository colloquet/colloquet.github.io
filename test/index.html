About us

Built upon a passion devoted to cake making and decorating. ZoiE is our platform to demonstrate and share this devotion with people alike as well as people who want to explore the joy this aspect of culinary art may bring. We have more than a decade of cake making and decorating experience for various occations and valuable lessons from chefs in Hong Kong, Japan and U.S.A. etc.

ZoiE offers a range of quality services including cake decorating classes for kids and adults, private parties (birthday, holiday, play dates, baby shower & etc.) custom designed cake, and team building event. We will also try our best to provide services that you may want that are not listed.

Our mission is to make ZoiE your baking specialist for every occasion.
